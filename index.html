Avg Exclusion.

Ways to exclude files & folder in Avg Antivirus.

  • Open your AVG Antivirus in your computer.
  • Click on Menu Button and open it.
  • Click on Setting Button.
  • Click on Exception Tab.
  • Click on Add Exception Button.
  • Click on Browse Button or enter the path in the box of the file or folder.
  • Select the folder or file which you want to exclude and Click on OK Button.
  • After the selection of path of file or folder click on add Exception Button.
  • Click on Blocked & Allowed apps Button.
  • Click on allowed app Button in Blocked & Allowed apps.
  • Click on select app manaually Button.
  • select the location of application and click on open Button.
